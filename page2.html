About Us

Lilac, a journey that starts from London. Our mission is to empower our customers to achieve their version of perfect skin.

From Farm to Face

We use fair trade and organic ingredients whenever possible in all of our formulas. Our mission is to create the healthiest cosmetics made with the highest quality ingredients. Lilac team work diligently to help customers find the perfect product or products for their skin type and concern.

Empowering and Inspiring

Everyday we strive to help customers to achieve beautiful, clear skin. We make skincare products to encourage women embracing their vulnerability. We encourage every customers to be their own kind of beautiful.
